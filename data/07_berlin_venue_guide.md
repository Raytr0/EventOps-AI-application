# Industrial Loft Berlin - Venue Guide
Location: Berlin, Germany
Capacity: Up to 1000 guests
Cost: Variable, starting at €12,000 for full buyout.
Features: Large open floor plans, exposed brick, high ceilings, built-in AV.
Dietary Policies: Complete bring-your-own vendor model. No in-house catering provided.
Theme Compatibility: Techno, industrial, modern art, underground.
