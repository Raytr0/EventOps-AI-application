# Seasonal Travel Advisories (Europe)

## Spring (March - May)
- Weather can be unpredictable; indoor backup venues are highly recommended for outdoor events.
- Peak travel season starts in May; book accommodations early.

## Summer (June - August)
- High heat expected. Venues without air conditioning should be avoided.
- Premium pricing in effect for all major cities.

## Autumn (September - November)
- Favorable weather for early autumn.
- Budget-friendly options become available in November.

## Winter (December - February)
- Holiday markets increase congestion in major cities.
- Transport delays are common due to snow in northern regions.
