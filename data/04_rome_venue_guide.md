# Villa Antica - Venue Guide
Location: Rome, Italy
Capacity: Up to 100 guests
Cost: Minimum €8,000 budget required.
Features: Rustic outdoor courtyard, wine cellar.
Dietary Policies: Flexible catering, Mediterranean cuisine focus. Accommodates all major allergies.
Music Policies: Strict noise curfew at 10:00 PM due to local residential zoning.
